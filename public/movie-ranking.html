🎬 My Top 3 Anime of All Time 🎬

A perfect mix of comedy, action, and emotion! 💛 Koro-sensei’s lessons about life, growth, and perseverance will stay with you forever. 🐙🎓💫

  • 📓 Death Note — A brilliant battle of wits between Light and L, wrapped in moral ambiguity and darkness. 🕯️💀
